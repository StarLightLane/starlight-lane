What the numbers mean

Reading your statement, honestly.

01 / INTERCHANGE

The real cost

Interchange is what the card networks (Visa, MC, Amex) actually charge. Every processor pays the same rate on the same card — there's no negotiating this part. It's usually 70 to 85 percent of your total fee.

02 / MARKUP

The part that varies

This is the processor's cut on top of interchange. Ours starts at 0.15%. Most tiered plans hide a 0.5% to 1.5% markup — which is why effective rates of 3% or higher are so common on your statement.

03 / FEES

Everything else

Statement fees. PCI non-compliance fees. Batch fees. Gateway fees. Monthly minimums. Cross-border fees. Sometimes $50 a month, sometimes $300. These are the junk charges we eliminate entirely.
