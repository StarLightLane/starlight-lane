01
Credit card processing

Interchange-plus, without the mystery.

Most processors use tiered or bundled pricing, which sounds simple but hides where your money actually goes. We use interchange-plus: you see the real card-network cost, plus a small, transparent markup. Every fee, every line, every month.

The pricing

Interchange + assessment + markup

Three line items. The card network's true cost (interchange), the brand fee (assessment, ~0.14%), and our transparent markup starting at 0.15%. Nothing bundled, nothing buried.

What's not there

No junk fees, no surprises

No PCI non-compliance charges for compliant merchants. No monthly minimums. No batch fees. No "qualified" vs "non-qualified" tier games. No statement fee, no early termination fee, no annual fee.

Hardware

EMV · NFC · virtual · e-commerce

Countertop terminals, mobile readers, handheld POS, virtual terminal for phone orders, hosted checkout pages, and direct API integration. Pick what fits your workflow. We'll ship it, configure it, and support it.

Funding

Next-day funding available

Standard settlement is T+1 business day. Same-day funding is available for qualifying merchants. You see exactly when the money is moving, because we show you — not because you had to call support to ask.

02
Holdback recovery

The money you forgot about.

A holdback is money your processor takes from your deposits as insurance against chargebacks. Sometimes it's justified. Usually it's overstated. Often, it's quietly forgotten. We find it, negotiate its release, and recover what's yours — even from relationships that ended years ago.

Start an audit →
Step 01

Reserve audit

Share your processing statements from the last 24 months. We identify every reserve, holdback, and contra-account balance — including ones your processor never flagged for you, or buried inside line items they know you don't read.

Step 02

Release negotiation

We contact the processor, document the case (chargebacks-to-volume ratio, time since last dispute, seasonality patterns), and negotiate an accelerated release schedule on your behalf.

Step 03

Dead-relationship recovery

Terminated an account years ago? Still owed money? Processors are legally required to return unclaimed funds, but they rarely advertise it. We file the paperwork, chase it down, and get it back.

The model

No recovery, no fee

Contingency-based. We take a percentage of what we actually recover, invoiced at settlement. If we find nothing, you pay nothing. No upfront, no retainer, no minimums.

The audit flow
1
Statements in

You send us the last 24 months of processing statements. Takes about ten minutes.

2
Audit out

Within three business days, we return a one-page report: every reserve, every balance, every holdback we found. Average recovery identified on a first audit: $14,300.

3
Release

We negotiate directly with the processor. Most releases close in 30 to 60 days. Funds wire straight to your account, not ours.

4
Settlement

A percentage of recovered funds, invoiced after the money lands. You know the number before we start. Nothing hidden.

03
AI integration

Every swipe is a signal.

Most processors treat your transaction data like exhaust — something to log and forget. We treat it like fuel. Our AI layer watches every authorization, surfaces patterns a human would miss, and hands you answers before you knew to ask.

See it in motion →
Pre-authorization

Real-time fraud scoring

Every transaction scored in under 80 milliseconds. Features include velocity, geographic anomaly, device fingerprint, BIN history, and merchant-level behavioral baselines. Blocks the obvious, passes the legitimate, flags the ambiguous for you to review.

Post-authorization

Chargeback prediction

After a transaction clears, we model the probability it will be disputed. High-risk transactions get flagged within 24 hours so you can preemptively refund, reach out, or adjust shipping — before the cardholder ever picks up the phone to call their bank.

Back-office

Auto-reconciliation

Deposits matched to invoices. Batch settlements categorized. Tax lines tagged. Partial payments and split settlements handled automatically. Export to QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, or plain CSV — whatever your bookkeeper already lives in.

Under the hood

Built on your data, not theirs

Each merchant gets its own model. We don't pool your transaction data with other businesses. SOC 2 Type II. PCI-DSS Level 1. Encrypted at rest and in transit. Always.
