Who we serve

Built for your business.

Generic processors charge everyone the same and expect them to be grateful. We tailor the setup — rate, funding schedule, hardware, software — to what your industry actually needs. Pick yours below, or scroll through to see how we think about each.

Typical rate1.60 – 1.90%

Retail

Brick-and-mortar · card-present

Boutiques, specialty stores, convenience, hardware, gift shops. Anywhere customers walk in, tap or swipe, and walk out with what they bought.

What usually hurts
  • Overpriced "free" terminal offers with 4-year equipment leases
  • PCI non-compliance fees on processors that don't help with compliance
  • Confusing tiered pricing where most transactions are "non-qualified"
What we do differently
  • Owned hardware, not leased — $199 terminals you keep forever
  • Interchange-plus pricing — no qualified/non-qualified games
  • Integrated inventory — via handheld POS or your existing system
  • Tap-to-pay included — Apple Pay, Google Pay, contactless cards
Typical rate1.80 – 2.10%

Restaurants

Full-service · QSR · bars · cafés

Food moves fast, margins are thin, and every minute your terminal hangs is a table not turned. You need a processor that gets hospitality — and has the funding schedule to match.

What usually hurts
  • Tip adjustments that break the auth and cost you chargebacks
  • T+2 funding when payroll and suppliers want money today
  • Terminal downtime during a Saturday night dinner rush
What we do differently
  • Same-day funding available — no extra fee for qualifying merchants
  • Clean tip adjustment flow — no auth surprises, no chargeback risk
  • POS integrations — Toast, Square, Clover, Lightspeed, TouchBistro
  • 4G-backup terminals — keeps running when your WiFi doesn't
Typical rate1.90 – 2.40%

E-commerce

Shopify · WooCommerce · custom

Card-not-present means higher interchange, higher fraud risk, and higher chargeback exposure. We bring the AI layer that turns that math back in your favor.

What usually hurts
  • Chargebacks that land 60 days after a sale and cost 3× the transaction
  • International fees that eat 1.5%+ on cross-border sales
  • Fraud filters that are either too loose or block your best customers
What we do differently
  • AI fraud scoring — 80ms latency, tunable thresholds
  • Chargeback prediction — 24-hour advance warning to intervene
  • Every major platform — Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, custom
  • Honest cross-border pricing — real interchange, no padding
Typical rate1.70 – 2.00%

Professional services

Legal · consulting · agencies · medical

Bigger tickets, lower volume, and most transactions are invoice-based. The processor questions that matter are different: can you send a clean invoice, and do recurring retainers work without a dozen workarounds?

What usually hurts
  • "Large ticket" surcharges on single transactions over $10k
  • Ugly, hard-to-pay invoice links that customers ignore
  • Manual reconciliation between invoices, deposits, and accounting software
What we do differently
  • No large-ticket penalties — one rate, every amount
  • Branded invoice links — your logo, custom confirmation, one-click pay
  • Recurring retainers built in — monthly or annual, with auto-receipts
  • QuickBooks / Xero auto-sync — every deposit matched to every invoice
Typical rate1.50 – 1.90%

SaaS & subscription

Recurring billing · digital products

The best SaaS businesses lose 5-10% of their ARR to "involuntary churn" — expired cards, failed charges, blocked attempts. Most of that's recoverable with the right setup.

What usually hurts
  • Failed renewals when customers get a new card (no account updater)
  • Hard declines on first attempt that would succeed with smart retry logic
  • Stripe fees eating 2.9% + 30¢ on transactions that should be under 2%
What we do differently
  • Account updater included — expired cards auto-refresh with the networks
  • Smart retry logic — ML-tuned retry times that recover 30%+ of declines
  • Rate drops for subscription volume — interchange is lower on recurring, you should benefit
  • Dunning emails built in — customer-friendly, your branding
Typical rate1.70 – 2.20%

Healthcare & medical

Practices · dental · specialty · vets

Patient payments are a mess of co-pays, deductibles, installment plans, and HSA/FSA cards — handled by processors who don't understand any of it. We do.

What usually hurts
  • HSA/FSA cards rejecting on processors that aren't coded correctly
  • No installment plan support — so uncollected balances stack up
  • Generic receipts that don't meet HSA/FSA documentation requirements
What we do differently
  • Full HSA/FSA card support — correct MCC coding, IIAS compliance
  • Installment plans built in — 3, 6, 12 months with automatic retry
  • Medical-compliant receipts — IIAS line-item detail where needed
  • PMS integrations — Dentrix, Open Dental, Eaglesoft, AthenaHealth, more
