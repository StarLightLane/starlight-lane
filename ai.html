02 / Post-authorization

Chargeback prediction.

After a transaction clears, we model the probability it will be disputed. Our system flags high-risk transactions within 24 hours so you can preemptively refund, adjust shipping, or reach out before the cardholder ever picks up the phone to call their bank.

24-hour forecast window — time to intervene before the dispute lands
Pattern matching — learns from your historical chargebacks, not a generic dataset
One-click actions — refund, flag, document, or ignore from the alert itself
Measurable ROI — merchants typically cut chargeback losses 40–60%
